Sara Poveda is a Spanish illustrator and programmer born in 30th June 1991.

Although she has studied the first year of an Illustration course in a Vocational School, she considers herself as a selftaught artist. She is keen on Illustration and Concept Art, and enjoys designing her own characters as well as drawing her favourite ones from different fandoms.

What she loves is to surpass herself with every new piece, experimenting with poses and colors.
